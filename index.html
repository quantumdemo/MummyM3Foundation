Our Mission & Vision

We are dedicated to providing compassionate support to the most vulnerable members of our community.

Empowering widows through skills acquisition and financial aid.

Providing counseling and support for widowers in need.

Ensuring dignity and care for the elderly in our community.

Offering education and shelter to orphaned children.

Protecting and nurturing children from disadvantaged backgrounds.

Assisting all vulnerable groups with essential services.

Providing support and resources for the less privileged.
